Quick ratios & notes

Mise en place: measure ingredients first, especially desserts.

Fish batter: plain flour, soda, beer — 1:1:1, salt. Optional lemon + malt vinegar.

Yorkshire: flour, eggs, milk — 1:1:1. Hot oil, 195°C, 18 mins.

Dressing: 3 parts oil, 1 sweetener, 1 mustard, 1 vinegar.

Chicken safety: cook chicken to at least 75°C.

Storage: follow your venue policy; stored times here are prep notes, not legal advice.
