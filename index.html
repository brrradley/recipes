Quick ratios & shift notes

Mise en place: measure ingredients first, especially desserts.

Fish batter: plain flour, soda, beer — 1:1:1, salt. Optional lemon + malt vinegar.

Yorkshire: plain flour, eggs, milk — 1:1:1. Hot oil, 195°C, 18 mins.

Dressing: 3 parts oil, 1 part sweetener, 1 part mustard, 1 part vinegar.

Chicken: cook to at least 75°C.
